Mysterious creatures (known simply as "Dukes") disguise themselves as shrubbery and run around having a jolly time. If discovered, they shed their disguise and laugh innocently at any human that happens to see them.
Fortunately for the hunter, there are a couple of tricks to finding dukes... and some of them are the dukes' own doing! Watch carefully for movement, since real shrubbery does not run about.
Also, be especially aware of the dukes' exploding decoys -- although the dukes designed them to destroy vegitation, they can easily backfire and destroy decoys, vegitation, and dukes alike!
